Who I am

Hi, I’m Anna and in my second year of getting a BFA for film production.  I have already worked on some student theater and film productions. Ultimately I want to work in film and comic production, with a focus on script/screenwriting and direction. I’m already interested in web design and digital media (I currently work for an IT department with a background in graphic design), and continue to work on my skills in those areas. I’m originally from the Chicago area with interests in film, comics, art, video games, science, history, theater, and writing. 

Available to hire in the Albuquerque area for film production, especially in the fields of: directing, screenwriting, grip and electric, and camera operation.
